Upcoming Events

The South Hill Honey Badgers is a Woodsball Team, which means we play in the great outdoors on a variety of fields.

We have two locations that we primarily play on, one located just outside of Greenwater, WA off Forest Service Road #70, and the other is private property in Buckley, WA.

Below is a map of the pinned location, where we play off of FS-70.

We also attend SuperGame in St. Paul, Oregon every year. This is one of the largest paintball events in the Pacific Northwest.

If you are interested in signing up, here is their website: https://supergamepaintball.com/oregon-sg.

We invite you to join us there! We are always on the blue team, so come and find us!
