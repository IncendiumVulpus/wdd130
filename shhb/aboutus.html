About Us

We are a group of friends who have been playing together for 15 years.

We started out as a few fathers with their kids once a month, playing paintball together in the woods.

We now regularly have groups of 20+ each month when we gather to play. We have 32 variant games that we play and on occasion we design our own scenario games.

Are you interested in playing with us?

If so, please reach out to us by email at: whaley004@gmail.com
